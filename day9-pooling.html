🪟 Pooling — Shrink the Map, Keep What Matters

2×2 pooling: four numbers in, one number out

Each coloured 2×2 block of the feature map collapses to a single value — max pooling keeps the strongest, average pooling takes the mean. The map halves in size; the important signal survives.
