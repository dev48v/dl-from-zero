⊕ XOR — the Problem That Demanded Hidden Layers

One straight line can't split XOR. Two layers can.

XOR is on when the inputs differ (🔴 corners) and off when they match (🔵 corners). No single straight line separates them — the 1969 result that froze AI for a decade. Flip on a hidden layer and watch the boundary bend.
